Koji Ichikawa is a New York-based Hair Stylist. He began his career in hairdressing at a hair salon when he was 18 years old. After 9 years, he moved to London to pursue a career in session styling in 2006. He served as the first assistant to the legendary hair stylist SAM MCKNIGHT for over 3 years, collaborating on editorials, major campaigns, and shows.

After assisting SAM MCKNIGHT, he started building his own portfolio as a session stylist. His work can be seen in publications such as VOGUE, HARPER’S BAZZAR, ELLE, NUMERO, L’OFFICIEL, MADAME FIGARO, NYLON, WWD, GQ, and SHOWstudio.

He has also worked with numerous celebrities including Lady Gaga, Amanda Seyfried, Arden Cho, Little Mix, Dean Caten, Dan Caten, Silvia Damiani, Steven Tyler, Ashanti, Charice, Russell Watson, Cody Fern, and Jared Leto.
